# Talent Acquisition Database 📊

A centralized database of Talent Acquisition (TA) professionals / HRs along with their contact details to help job seekers connect with the right people faster and smarter.

---

## 📌 About the Project

This repository is designed to serve as a **useful resource for job seekers**, especially in the IT and DevOps domains, by providing:

- 👥 List of HRs / Talent Acquisition Specialists  
- 📧 Contact details (Email / LinkedIn where applicable)  
- 🏢 Company information  
- 📍 Location details  

The goal is to **bridge the gap between candidates and recruiters** and make job searching more efficient.

---

## 🚀 Use Cases

- Directly reach out to recruiters  
- Improve chances of getting interview calls  
- Build professional networking connections  
- Explore opportunities across companies  

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

- This data is collected from **publicly available sources**.  
- Please use this information responsibly and professionally.  
- Avoid spamming or misuse of contact details.
